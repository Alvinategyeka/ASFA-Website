About The Arm Stretch Foundation Africa Ltd

The Arm Stretch Foundation Africa Ltd is a non-governmental organization situated in Uganda, Kampala, with a vision to grow across Africa. Our name, "Arm Stretch," reflects our commitment to extending a helping hand to the vulnerable in partnership with various groups and well-wishers.

Our Slogan

"Transforming Dreams to Reality" through our three alphabetic guides:

  • F - Face: We face the situation.
  • S - Speak: We speak about it.
  • A - Act: We take action (i.e., remedies).

Our Vision

Being the life transformer in the livelihood of the hopeless and voiceless, building strong self-reliance through hand and train skills within the community.

Our Mission

Promoting high self-esteem built through hope, love, and care within underrated communities.

Our Values

  • P: Promoting hope, love, and care.
  • E: Empowering the vulnerable.
  • B: Building strong integrity and self-esteem.
  • A: Attitude instillation in the vulnerable.
