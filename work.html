Our Work

At The Arm Stretch Foundation Africa Ltd, we are committed to transforming the lives of vulnerable communities through targeted initiatives and sustainable solutions.

Our Target Groups

  • Children school dropouts
  • Hunger and malnutrition
  • Early unwanted pregnancies and marriages
  • Unprivileged communities

Our Services

  • Sensitization campaigns to eliminate socio-cultural and gender barriers.
  • Provision of scholastic materials and basic needs.
  • Counseling and nutritional lessons.
  • Agricultural and hand-based skills training (e.g., tailoring, catering, computer skills, mechanics, carpentry).
